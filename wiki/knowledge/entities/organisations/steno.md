# Steno

relates_to::[[../../sources/prd-demand-letter-generator.md]]

Steno is a legaltech company that serves thousands of litigation attorneys. It is building the **demand letter generator** as a new AI-powered product offering. The tool is intended to increase deal closure rates with law firms and improve net revenue retention through attorney time savings.
