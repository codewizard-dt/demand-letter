# Patrick Donahue

relates_to::[[../../sources/aaa-insurance-demand-letter-pat-donahue.md]]

Personal injury claimant in the sample demand letter. Occupation: RV technician (physically demanding role requiring work underneath vehicles — directly impacted by spinal injuries). Injured in a rear-end collision on June 6, 2025 on Highway 67/8 East, El Cajon, CA when Larry L. Lawhorn failed to stop and struck Donahue's vehicle from behind.

**Medical summary:** multilevel cervical, thoracic, and lumbar injuries including disc protrusions and extrusions (most significant at L4-L5 and L5-S1), cervical and lumbar radiculitis, and facet joint pathology. Ongoing pain rated severe with movement as of April 2026 follow-up. Recommended next steps: lumbar medial branch block, physical therapy, cervical MRI.

**Damages:** $8,625.58 in billed medical specials; $50,000+ in future medical reserve; $100,000 in general damages (pain and suffering). Represented by relates_to::[[faby-rivera.md]] at relates_to::[[../organisations/stalwart-law-group.md]].
